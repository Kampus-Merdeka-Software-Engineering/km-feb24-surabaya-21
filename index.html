    Business Insights

    1. The top 5 most sold pizza types are classic_dlx, bbq_ckn, hawaiian, pepperoni, and thai_ckn.
    2. The five least sold pizza types are brie_Carre, mediterrraneo, calabrese, spinach_supr, and soppressata.
    3. Pizza Place's 2015 sales trends revealing fluctuating revenue, hitting a low in October and "Brie_Carre" consistently underperformed.
    4. Every month, L-size pizzas are the most popular.

    Business Recommendations

    1. Based on pizza sales data by size, we can remove the least or low selling pizza sizes and maximize the pizza sizes whose sales are most in demand, namely Small, Medium, and Large.
    2. Pizza sales fluctuate every month, the highest is in July while the lowest is in October. Based on this data, we can maximize sales in the following 6 months of the year so that sales each month do not fluctuate anymore.
    3. The most sold pizza type is classic_dlx while the least is brie_carre type. Based on the data, we can maximize the sales of the lowest menu by creating a bundling menu with the top 5 most ordered menus.
